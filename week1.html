Research on Body Augmentation

What is body augmentation? Can we find different categories to differentiate different kinds of augmentation? These were the first questions that we asked ourselves. Below is the definition according to technopedia.

Human augmentation is generally used to refer to technologies that enhance human productivity or capability, or that somehow add to the human body. Modern advancements in many areas of IT have led to a greater variety of implants and other technologies that could be classed as human augmentation. Human augmentation may also be called human 2.0.

Based on the kind of references we found, we did sort the references into a mood board. Three layers of information are included. On the first level, we marked each project with either a R=real or a F=fictional. Then we designed a matrix 2x2 where we put "physical augmentation" and "cognitive augmentation" on the x-axis and we put "enhancement" and "distortion" on the y-axis.

Body Culture for the West (Central Europe)

Judaism-Christianity
Nature is the result of God’s will, dominance of man over nature, where the body is the temple of the soul.

Out of the ground the LORD God formed every beast of the field and every bird of the sky, and brought them to the man to see what he would call them; and whatever the man called a living creature, that was its name.

Genesis 2:19
You must know that your body is a temple of the Holy Spirit, who is within—the Spirit you have received from God. You are not your own. So glorify God in your body.

Paulus, 1 Corinthians 6:19–20

Ancient Greece
Nature as a background to man’s activity. Man and nature should cohabit in harmony.

This world, which is the same for all, no one of gods or men has made. But it always was and will be: an ever-living fire, with measures of it kindling, and measures going out.

Eraclitus, Fragments
While he is drinking he beholds himself reflected in the mirrored pool—and loves; loves an imagined body which contains no substance, for he deems the mirrored shade a thing of life to love.

Ovid, Metamorphosis Book 3

Modern Phylosophy
Substance Dualism. This philosophy states that the mental can exist outside of the body, and the body cannot think. Following phylosophy of Cartesio.

Body Culture for the East (China, Japan)

Taoism

“昔者庄周梦为蝴蝶，栩栩然蝴蝶也，自喻适志与！不知周也。俄然觉，则蘧蘧然周也。不知周之梦为蝴蝶与，蝴蝶之梦为周与？”
《庄子·齐物论》

Once upon a time Zhuang Zhou dreamed to be a butterfly. He was roaring in the air with free will. After a while, he was awaken. He couldn’t tell whether it was him dreaming to be a butterfly or the butterfly was dreaming to be him.

Zhuangzi， 'butterfly passage' of chapter 2

Confucianism

使目非是无欲见也，使耳非是无欲闻也。使口非是无欲言也，使心非是无欲虑也。及至其致好之也，目好之五色，耳好之五声，口好之五味，心利之有天下。
《荀子·劝学第一

To let eyes not see those who were not supposed to be seen, to let ears not hear those who were not supposed to be heard, to let mouses not say those who were not supposed to be said, to let hearts not seek those who were not supposed to be sought. Then comes the harmony. Eyes seeking five colors, ears seeking five sounds, mouths seeking five flavours, thus hearts will do good to the society.

Xunzi, Encouraging learning (Section 1)

Mohism

故圣人为衣服，适身体，和肌肤而足矣，非荣耳目而观愚 民也
《墨子·辞过第六》

So when the saint seeks for clothes, they are looking for those which fits the body and has the best relationship with the skin. Clothes for saint is not for the pleasure of eyes.

Mozi, Indulgence in excess (Section 6)

Washida Kiyokazu

「第二の皮膚としての衣服」という見方を転倒し、身体を第一の衣服だと見る考え。...衣服は熱いシャワーなどと同様に境界画定の効果を持つという見解である。
「モードの迷宮」鷲田清一

Skin is the first boundary of the body, clothes and hot spring are also defining the boundaries of body same as the skin does.

“Modo no Meikyu”，Washida Kiyokazu

Hai Zi

Zhuang Zi is washing his hands in the water
over his palms a spread of silence
Zhuang Zi is washing his body in the water
his body a bolt of cloth
Clinging to the cloth are sounds
adrift on the water

Zhuang Zi wants to blend in
with the moon-gazing beasts
Bones grow inch by inch
like branches
above and below his navel

Perhaps Zhuang Zi is me
He touches the bark
and feels close
to his own body
Close and agonized
the moon touches me

as if I’m naked
nakedly
enter and exit

Mother is a door, gently open to me

Thinking about the past life，Hai Zi
